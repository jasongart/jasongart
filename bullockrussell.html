Bullock Russell

Art Direction / Graphic Design

Marketing materials for a leading real estate brokerage in South Orange County, California.

Bullock Russell Real Estate Services is a real estate brokerage in South Orange County. While many in real estate don't use red for their brand Bullock Russell has siezed upon the opportunity to do so in order to gain brand recognition (with success) in a field of competitors relying on more neutral tones along with blues and greens.

Many Bullock Russell agents are quick to credit their marketing materials as the reason they've been able to land clients, citing the design as among the best in the business.
