Logo Animation

Motion Graphics / Graphic Design

Logo designs set to animation with Adobe After Effects.

Dot's Tots is the maker of custom blankets and other products for newborns and infants. In addition to designing the logo type the logo animation is playful with the bouncing of the dots, the chaotic flying around of the dots and the rocking of the letters like a rocking chair or cradle (or at least how cradles were once upon a time). It's all designed to resemble the playfulness of children as well as caring for them.

Bullock Russell Real Estate Services is a real estate brokerage in Orange County, California. Often refered to in short hand as BR the animation starts of that way as a tease and with "Real Estate" in the tagline subtly changing to "Real Estate Services". The transitions between the "tease" and the actual logo are as seamless as possible with the rotating BR designed as the star compared to the bottom half, which also works, but should not be the viewers focus.

Resurrection OC is a church in Orange County, California. The logo was created by another designer, and is well structured for animation. This was my first ever attempt at animating a logo and the opportunity presented itself as a fun challenge at the time. The symbolism of the cross is central to Christian faith so it seemed appropriate to me that the logo forms from it, because the church itself (or any church) doesn't exist without it.
