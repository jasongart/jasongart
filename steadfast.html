Steadfast Capital Markets Group

Art Direction / Graphic Design

Marketing materials for the financial services division of a commercial real estate company.

When Steadfast Capital Markets Group (SCMG) formed in 2009 the company had no branding and no marketing materials. Within a few years SCMG had a vast library of materials and an identity etched into the minds of colleagues and contemporaries alike. Those receiving marketing for SCMG's products recognized Steadfast for having some of the best looking and clearest to understand materials in the alternative investment business.
