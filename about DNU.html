About Me

I am a UX/UI and Graphic Designer based out of Orange County, California. With over a decade of experience as both an in-house and freelance creative I have helped to establish with my employers a reputation for having the best marketing materials in their field, engaged problem solving that requires professional growth and development, and collaborated with marketing and creative teams to produce the best possible solutions for problems facing my employers and clients.

My success as a Designer stems from two things:

This is something I keep at the top of my mind with every project I take on.
